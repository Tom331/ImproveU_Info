Privacy Policy

ImproveU  ·  Last updated: March 2, 2026

Your tracking data (scores, notes, and categories) is stored only on your device and is never uploaded, shared, or sold.

1. What We Collect

We collect the minimum necessary to provide the app:

  • Email address — collected when you create an account or sign in with Google or Apple. Used solely for authentication (login, password reset).

We do not collect your scores, notes, category data, or any other personal tracking information. That data lives on your device only.

2. How Your Data Is Stored

  • Account data (email) is stored securely with Supabase, our authentication provider. See Supabase's Privacy Policy.
  • All tracking data (daily scores, notes, categories, streaks) is stored locally on your device using SQLite. It never leaves your device.

3. Third-Party Services

ImproveU uses Supabase for authentication only. Your email address is transmitted to Supabase over an encrypted connection when you sign in or create an account. We do not use any analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

4. Sign In with Apple / Google

If you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive only the email address associated with your account. We do not receive or store any other profile information from those providers.

5. Your Rights

  • Delete your account — you can permanently delete your ImproveU account directly in the app (Profile → Delete Account). This removes your account from our authentication provider and wipes all local data on your device.
  • Clear local data — you can reset all local tracking data at any time (Profile → Clear Local Data) without deleting your account.
  • Log out — you can sign out at any time from the Profile tab.

6. Children's Privacy

ImproveU is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at support@improveu.app.
