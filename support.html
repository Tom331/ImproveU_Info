FAQ

Is my data private?
Yes. All your tracking data is stored locally on your device and never uploaded to our servers.

How do I delete my data?
You can delete your account and all associated data from the Profile screen inside the app.

I forgot my password — what do I do?
Tap "Forgot Password" on the login screen and we'll send you a reset link.
